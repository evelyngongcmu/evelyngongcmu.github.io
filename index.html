Hello. I am B P Junior Faculty Chair and Assistant Professor of Operations Management at Carnegie Mellon University’s Tepper School of Business. Prior to joining Tepper, I completed my PhD in Operations Research at MIT.

My main research lies in artificial intelligence for supply chain and sustainability. I am also interested in assortment optimization, pure exploration, algorithms and data-driven decision making in general.
