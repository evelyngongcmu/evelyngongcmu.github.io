Evelyn Xiao-Yue Gong


Assistant Professor


Tepper School of Business

Carnegie Mellon University

Hello. I am an Assistant Professor of Operations Management at Carnegie Mellon University’s Tepper School of Business.

My main research lies in artificial intelligence for supply chain and sustainability. I am also interested in assortment optimization, pure exploration, algorithms and data-driven decision making in general.

I completed my PhD in Operations Research at MIT, advised by Professor David Simchi-Levi and Professor Jim Orlin. During my PhD, I completed several internships in the industry, including at Google Research, at Microsoft Research AI, at HelloFresh and at D.E. Shaw.

I am highly motivated by real problems and applications.
